
6. Ancient Texts & Cultural Encoding

- Sacred Agriculture: Texts like the Vrikshayurveda contain spiritual planting guidance.
- Chant as Memory: Filipino Hudhud chants encode terrace farming knowledge.
- Story as Storage: Native American legends explain sustainable practices (e.g., Three Sisters planting).
- Oral Archives: Ancient songs and rituals act as living libraries.

Encoded ecological knowledge passed through chant and story.
