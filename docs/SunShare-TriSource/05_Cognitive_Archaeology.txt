
4. Ethnolinguistics & Cognitive Archaeology

- Songlines: Aboriginal chants encode geography and survival data.
- Memory Architecture: Monuments like Stonehenge may have functioned as memory palaces.
- Archaeoacoustics: Petroglyphs often located at echo-rich sites, suggesting sound guided sacred art.
- Ritual Alignment: Structures aligned with solstices acted as timekeeping tools for agricultural knowledge.

Ancient knowledge systems were embedded in symbol, chant, and spatial design.
